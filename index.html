Welcome to AFES.

This is an automated Finite Element solver, including mesh adaptivity and optimization, for partial differential equations based in FEniCS/DOLFIN. Right now, we are very much still in the alpha stages of development, so not much information will be provided and the repo is currently private. Once we reach beta the repo will become public and users will be able to pull from the repo.
